FACILITIES & INFRASTRUCTURE

OPERATION THEATRES

The Operation Theatre (OT) complexes have dedicated OTs for Urology & Kidney Transplantation, Laparascopic Surgery and Orthopaedic Surgery. The OT complex is isolated with a sterile corridor; entry is through an air-shower system. Each theatre has an independent laminar airflow mechanism with filters to maintain independent laminar,complexes have dedicated OTs for Urology

HEMO DIALYSIS UNIT

The Dialysis Unit at KIET Hospitals offers exceptional care and treatment options, featuring the latest technology and comes with high quality dialysis machines, experienced dialysis staff and personal attention to patients.

The department has a well-equipped Hemo-dialysis Unit consisting of 10 machines from world renowned Fresenius 4008 S and 5008 S therapy machines. Water for dialysis is provided by Reverse Osmosis (RO).featuring the latest technology and comes with high quality

CRRT

The department has state-of-the-art intensive care facility for patients with kidney injury and MODS. This facility comes with Continuous Renal Replacement Therapies (CRRT) provision for dialysis patients. Continuous Renal Replacement Therapies (CRRT) is dialysis treatment that is provided as a continuous therapy. Intermittent dialysis treatments are treatments that are provided for brief intervals, usually every day or every 2-3 days as required

EMERGENCY UNIT

The Emergency unit at KIET Hospital is a 24-hour, state-of-the-art medical centre, which caters to all types of accident victims and emergency cases. It is equipped with the latest infrastructure. Medical supplies are available at all times from the 24-hour in-house pharmacy ably supported by the most experienced team with years of experience

LABORATORY AND DIAGNOSTIC SERVICES

KIET Hospitals has a wide range of laboratory investigations necessary for patient care. The laboratory is equipped with automated biochemistry analyzers, hormone analyzers etc.

ICU

The hospital has various intensive care units with total of 16 beds consisting of ultra-modern equipment and infrastructure to meet critical and emergency situations and provide special care to the patients. ICCU, Surgical ICU, Kidney Transplant ICU.
